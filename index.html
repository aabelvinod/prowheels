Who We Are

Developing Champions on Wheels

Located in the heart of Thanissery Kallada, Prowheels Coaching Center is more than just a skating rink. We are a community dedicated to the sport of Roll Ball and Roller Skating.

From beginners putting on their first pair of skates to elite athletes competing at the national level, our certified coaches provide personalized training to ensure every student reaches their full potential. We focus on technique, speed, agility, and sportsmanship.

  • Professional Certification
  • Safety First Approach
  • State-of-the-art Track
